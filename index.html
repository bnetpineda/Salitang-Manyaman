Instructions

Start:
You begin with an empty field where you can input your guesses. The secret word is not visible to you initially.
Guessing:
Input a five-letter word guess into the provided space. The letters may turn different colors to provide feedback on your guess: Green: Correct letter in the correct position. Yellow: Correct letter, but in the wrong position. Gray/Black: Incorrect letter.
Feedback:
Use the feedback from each guess to refine your next attempts. For example, if the word is "BRAVE," and you guessed "RAVEN," you might see two green, one yellow, and two gray/black indicators.
Strategy:
Use a mix of common words and variations to deduce the secret word. Pay attention to the feedback and adjust your guesses accordingly.
Winning: You win by guessing the correct word within six attempts. The game typically ends after either a correct guess or after six attempts, revealing the secret word.
Losing:
If you can't guess the word within six attempts, the game ends, and the secret word is revealed.
Tips:
Start with Common Letters: Begin your guesses with common letters to quickly eliminate possibilities. Analyze Feedback: Use the feedback from each guess to strategically narrow down the options. Think Outside the Box: Consider less common words or variations if your initial guesses aren't successful.
